ℹ️ About STAB

Student Teacher Appointment Booking (STAB) is a modern platform that streamlines scheduling between students and teachers. Book appointments, manage availability, and track status in real-time — all from one place.

📖 How to Use

1Register as a Student or Teacher
2Students: Select a teacher, pick a date & time, and book
3Teachers: Set availability, approve/reject/postpone requests
4Get email notifications for appointment updates
5View & edit your profile anytime

📜 Terms & Conditions

1. Users must provide accurate information during registration.

2. Profile photos must be appropriate. Inappropriate content will lead to account suspension.

3. Appointments must be booked at least 24 hours in advance.

4. Teachers reserve the right to approve, reject, or postpone appointments.

5. Personal data is stored securely via Firebase and will not be shared with third parties.

6. Users must not misuse the platform for any purpose other than academic appointments.

7. The admin reserves the right to modify or remove any user account.
